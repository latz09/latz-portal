How it works
Four steps that work the same whether you're hiring a builder or building it yourself.
01

Plan & budget

Tell us about your build. Pick a floor plan or share your own. We talk through your land, budget, and what your local trades will handle.

02

Design & quote

We finalize the blueprints and give you a firm price for your log package. No moving targets, no hidden fees — what you see is what ships.

Source & deliver

Your logs are sourced, prepared, and delivered directly to your build site on our own truck. You don't pay a middleman to coordinate.
