Order Mgmt Manual — no dashboard, no shipping labels, no inventory tracking

Shopify Development Workflow

Dev Store Created via Shopify Partners (free). No bank account needed during development.
At Launch Dev store transfers to Chris. He creates Shopify account, enters bank/billing, picks $39/mo Basic plan.
API Keys Storefront API keys come from dev store. Update Vercel env vars when store transfers to Chris before going live.
Setup Included Jordan sets up entire Shopify account — products, payment processing, shipping settings. Chris gets a fully running store handed to him. Walkthrough included.
💡
Why custom rod builder can't use native Shopify Shopify's variant system maxes at 100 variants per product. A fully configurable custom rod with all length, blank, color, finish, guide, and hardware combinations blows past that immediately. The rod builder must stay custom on the frontend and submit to Shopify checkout at the end.
💡
Vercel Free Tier Caveat Vercel's Hobby (free) tier is technically for non-commercial use per their terms. In practice most small businesses run on it without issue. If Vercel ever enforces or traffic spikes, upgrade to Pro ($20/mo) is instant — no migration needed. Current traffic profile is well within free tier limits.

Client Communication Style

Sanity → Shopify Migration Note

V1 had everything in Sanity including all commerce. Jordan now recognizes that Sanity isn't ideal for commerce — it creates manual order management with no native checkout, shipping, or inventory tools. The v2 architecture fixes this by putting commerce in Shopify where it belongs and keeping content in Sanity where it's strong. The pitch to Chris: "same CMS you already know for content, plus a real commerce engine for the business side."

Don't Tie Payment to Sales

Initially considered tying the final third payment to "first few sales" — decided against it. Jordan has zero control over whether Chris promotes the site, and defining "first few sales" gets messy. All three payments are tied to deliverables Jordan controls: signing, midpoint milestone, launch. Clean, fair, professional.
