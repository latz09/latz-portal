Rod Builder Configurator
CMS managed
⚑ This is the most intricate part of the site. The rod builder configurator involves multiple option types (application, length, power/action, handle material, thread color, guide options, grip style, etc.) with additive pricing. All option types and step flows will be mapped out collaboratively to determine the best UX approach for the rebuild. The block is intentionally left blank here — detailed wireframing happens during the design phase. Custom rod orders check out through Shopify via draft orders.
