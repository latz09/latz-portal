Reduced Scope — Two Pages
Based on budget feedback, the build is now two pages: a single-scroll Home page and a standalone Birthday Parties page. The former About, FAQ, Memberships, and Plan Your Visit pages are now sections within Home, and the separate pre-launch page is gone — its "opening soon" messaging and email capture fold into Home, which launches early in a stripped-down state and expands as opening approaches. Birthday Parties stays standalone to hold its local-SEO target. Nothing about positioning, the Rhomb handoff, or the content itself changed — only how many URLs it lives across.
✍️ Brand Voice & Messaging
Brand voice, messaging, and wording across the site will be coordinated with Allison & Alex Hewett and Pookie Rosa of Pookie Rosa Marketing, who leads brand voice and marketing strategy. Headlines and copy shown throughout these wireframes are placeholders to illustrate structure and intent — final wording comes through that coordination.
Provisional Colors & Fonts — Not Locked
Logo and branding are still in progress (Allison expects the logo in 1–2 weeks). The navy / marigold / teal / warm-cream palette and the Fraunces + DM Sans pairing shown here are a provisional placeholder chosen to lean into the brand's actual positioning — calm, family-owned, "no screens," not a loud arcade. They are easy to swap once the logo lands. Final palette and type are flagged in the Discussion Checklist.
