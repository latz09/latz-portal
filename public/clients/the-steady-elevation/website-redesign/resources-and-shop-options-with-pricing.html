01 What Changed
Stephanie,

Based on our May 27 call, this is a confirmation of where we landed — not a new pitch. You looked at the three configurations from the original proposal and decided you don't need on-site payment processing yet. Every resource will link out to where it already lives.

That's a real simplification. Removes the storefront build, the file-delivery layer, the sales tax question, all of it. What's left is the part you actually need right now: a Resources page that lets people get to your stuff, and event interest capture so you can build a list before announcing dates.

Final number is $625. Details below.

02 What's Being Built
Item 1

Resources Page

  • Lives on both Individual and Business sides of the site
  • Card grid layout — each card has image, title, description, button
  • Every button links to an external destination (Amazon, Eventbrite, direct PDF download, anywhere with a URL)
  • No on-site payments, no checkout, no file uploads, no auto-delivery
  • You add and manage every resource through your CMS
  • Conditional nav: Resources link only appears on a side when at least one resource exists for that side — no empty pages, no broken-looking nav
Item 2

Event Interest Capture

  • Toggle per event in CMS — you decide which events show the form
  • Visitors enter their email on the event card and click "Notify Me"
  • Emails are stored on the event in the CMS
  • You get a notification email whenever someone signs up — no need to check the CMS manually
  • CSV export from the CMS anytime
03 How This Compares to the Original Proposal

The original proposal had three configurations ranging from $800 to $2,000 — all of which included building payment processing into your site (Lemon Squeezy or Stripe). Based on our May 27 conversation, you decided that on-site payment processing isn't needed yet. Everything you want to sell can link to where it already lives — your book on Amazon, paid events on Eventbrite, free resources as direct downloads. That removes most of the build complexity and brings the price down significantly.

Nothing about this build prevents adding on-site payments later. If your needs change in 6–12 months, we can add Stripe or Lemon Squeezy integration as a separate engagement. You're not locked into anything.
