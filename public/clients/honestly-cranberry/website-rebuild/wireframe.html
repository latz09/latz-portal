Design North Star — Restraint
They explicitly liked their old GoDaddy site for being simple and clean — that's the bar. Don't over-design just because we can. Two things to hold onto: (1) Photo-led, story-forward. Hero and the Our Story band are committed to real family / farm photography — the design is built around the photos, so we need the actual images in hand before those blocks lock. (2) Reviews do two jobs in two places — Yotpo lives on product pages (conversion), Google + curated quotes live on the homepage (brand trust). Do not consolidate them into one review wall.

  • ✅
    Reviews Split — Two Systems, Two Jobs — Yotpo (product-level, ~1,000 reviews) lives on product pages and drives conversion. Google + curated quotes (brand-level) live on the homepage. They are NOT consolidated.
  • ✅
    Claim-Led Spine — Every page earns its order: claim → proof → product → story → proof again. Single-ingredient claim leads.
  • ✅
    Wisconsin Foodie Video — Full YouTube embed on Home and Our Story. The whole episode/segment plays in place; YouTube handles hosting and the player. The credibility no competitor can claim.
  • ✅

  • ✅
