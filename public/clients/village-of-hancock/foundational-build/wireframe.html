✓ Confirmed Decisions
  • ✅
    5-Page Scope — Home, About, Contact, Agendas & Minutes, Ordinances. No calendar, no news bulletin, no payment system in this build.
  • ✅
    CMS for Documents & Directory — Clerk manages agendas, minutes, ordinances, and board directory via Sanity. No need to call Jordan for routine updates.
  • ✅
    No Online Payments — Not in scope for this build. Future upsell.
  • ✅
    Click-to-Call Phone Numbers — All phone numbers are tap-to-call on mobile. Critical for 60+ audience.
  • ✅
    Download Links Open PDFs in New Tab — Agendas, minutes, and ordinances all open in a new tab rather than force-downloading.
  • ✅
    Jordan Writes Copy — Final copy for Home and About comes from Jordan based on Jillian's bullet points. Not the clerk's job.
  • ✅
    Domain — villageofhancock.org. Jordan handles DNS transfer at launch.
