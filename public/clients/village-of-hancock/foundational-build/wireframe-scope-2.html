What does the board want to call this page in the nav and as the page title?

Archive — Worth Adding?
Open question
Is an archive even needed here? A simple chronological feed (newest first, scroll down for older) might be enough — especially in year one, when post volume is low. If posts pile up over time and the feed gets unwieldy, a year-by-year archive can be added later as a small add-on.
Decision — Archive Approach
  • Option A — Chronological only, no archive. Simplest. Newest posts at top, older posts below. Fine for low post volume. Build it now; add an archive later if it ever feels needed.
  • Option B — Chronological + "load more" or pagination. Show 10–15 posts by default, button to reveal older. Lightweight middle ground.
  • Option C — Chronological + year filter from day one. Year buttons appear above the feed (2026, 2025, etc.) as posts accumulate.
Jordan's lean: Option A. Hancock isn't publishing dozens of posts a month — a chronological feed will be readable for years. Easier to add an archive later than to over-engineer up front.
