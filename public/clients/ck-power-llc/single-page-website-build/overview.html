📐 May 8, 2026 — CMS vs Expansion — Don't Conflate

Two different concepts got blurred on the May 8 call. Documenting clearly so future-Jordan and Chris are aligned:

CMS (included at $1,375): Self-service editing for text and images on existing sections. Chris can change the hero headline, swap a Trimlight photo, update phone number, edit service area list, etc. He CANNOT add new pages, new sections, or fundamentally restructure the site through the CMS.

Expansion architecture (paid add-ons): Future pages and sections built by Jordan. Solar landing page, photo gallery, blog, dedicated Trimlight landing page — all of these are paid Phase 2+ work. The site is engineered to plug these in cleanly without rebuilds, but they are billable scope, not CMS edits.

Make this distinction explicit in the proposal language. Make it explicit during CMS training. Don't let Chris discover the difference 6 months in and feel surprised.
