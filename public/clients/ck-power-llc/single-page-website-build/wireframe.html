📖 CMS (Content Editor) vs. Future Expansion
Two Different Things — Don't Get Them Confused
On our call we talked about both the CMS and future expansion. They sound similar but they're not the same thing. Here's the plain-language breakdown.
✅ Included — $1,375 Package
CMS / Content Editor
A simple editor that lets you update what's already on the site without calling Jordan.
What you can do
  • Edit text on existing sections (hero, services, about, etc.)
  • Swap out photos as you build a portfolio of real installs
  • Update your phone number, email, or service area list
  • Tweak service descriptions or pricing language
  • Day-to-day edits — no developer needed
⚡ Add-On — Future Paid Work
Future Site Expansion
Adding entirely new pages or sections to the site as your business grows. These are separate paid projects built by Jordan.
Examples
  • A dedicated solar service page (when you add solar)
  • A photo gallery section with multiple categories
  • Individual service landing pages for each offering
  • A blog or news section
  • A booking or scheduling system
The site's architecture is built so expansion is clean — no rebuild needed when you want to add a new page. But the CMS itself doesn't add new pages. Think of the CMS as a way to keep what's there fresh; expansion is when you want something new that doesn't exist yet. Both are easy to do — they're just billed differently. The CMS comes with your $1,375 package; expansions are quoted as separate small projects when you're ready.
