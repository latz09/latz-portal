A Note on Photos

📷
Quality matters more than quantity — here's what to look for. Good photos can make or break a website. The tips below will help you send the most useful ones. That said, send everything you have — we'll sort through it.
Photo Tips
✅ Great shots
  • Horizontal / landscape orientation
  • Well-lit — outdoors or bright natural light
  • Shows people, places, or community life
  • Lakes, park, village landmarks
  • Community events — street dance, gatherings
  • Seasonal — all four seasons welcome
⚠️ Try to avoid
  • Screenshots from Facebook or social media
  • Very dark or blurry shots
  • Heavily filtered or edited photos
  • Portrait / vertical phone photos (for hero use)
  • Tiny file sizes — send the originals

Domain Info

🌐
We'll need access to villageofhancock.org to connect it to the new site. Don't worry — this is a routine step and I'll walk you through everything. You stay in control of the domain at all times.
What We Need to Know
  • 🔍
    Who is your domain registrar?
    This is the company where villageofhancock.org is registered — GoDaddy, Namecheap, Network Solutions, and Google Domains (now Squarespace Domains) are common ones. The best way to find out: check your inbox for the original domain registration confirmation email, or search your email for "villageofhancock.org." You can also type the domain into who.is or do a quick Google search for "whois villageofhancock.org" — it'll tell you right away. Getting familiar with where it lives now will make the connection step much smoother later.
  • 🔑
    Login credentials
    Once you've tracked down the registrar, locate your login credentials and keep them handy. We'll reach out when it's time to connect the domain and figure out the quickest path from there. The domain stays yours — nothing gets transferred.
