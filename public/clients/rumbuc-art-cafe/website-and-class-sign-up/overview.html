Discovery Questions to Get Answered

The Big Stuff (Ask Early)

  • Is this you and Derek together? (Almost certainly yes. Confirm. Only ask after she mentions him.)
  • What's the legal/brand name? Have you filed an LLC? Determines domain strategy and branding direction.
  • Where will the location be? Downtown Appleton vs. strip mall changes everything about conversion math.
  • Do you have a lease yet? Yes = ~90 days out. No = 6+ months out.
  • What's your target open date? Drives every timeline decision.
  • Have you set aside a range for the website piece? Don't ask "what's your budget?" directly. This phrasing is better.
  • Where are you with branding — logo, colors, fonts? Done, in progress, not started? Extending DZigns or building new? Does Derek want to do it himself?
  • What's your plan for photography? You probably don't have class shots yet. Raise this as a real issue to solve together.

The Functional Stuff (Site Mechanics)

  • Class format mix? Monthly multi-session series, one-off workshops, private parties — all three?
  • Payment model — full up-front, or deposit + balance? Affects Stripe setup complexity.
  • Class capacity? Standard is 6. Confirm to build the right signup logic.
  • Cancellation / refund policy? Affects signup flow + admin UI.
  • Waitlist when classes fill? Big UX decision — adds complexity but adds real revenue recovery.
  • Selling physical product online too? Derek already does on Etsy. Consolidate? Or keep Etsy separate?
  • Multiple instructors, or just Derek? Affects CMS — do we need an instructor model?
  • Email marketing — do you already use anything? Mailchimp, etc., or fresh start with Resend?
  • Square / Toast POS for the café? Or is the café side walk-in only and not on the website at all?
