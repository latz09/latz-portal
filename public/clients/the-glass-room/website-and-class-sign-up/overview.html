Class Signup Spec — The Hero Build

🎯
This is the feature that justifies the project Sam knows exactly what she wants. The spec below came directly from her on the call — capture it verbatim in the proposal so she sees we listened.
Event Listing

List all upcoming events in chronological order

Each event clickable → dynamic event page. Show enough info on the list to filter by interest before clicking through.

Event Detail Page (per class)

Each event page includes:

Month design (visual + name) · Date(s) and time(s) · Beginner-friendly snippet · Short description · Monthly calendar view with Wednesdays highlighted · Skill level field (default: beginner) · Seats available out of 8 (CMS-editable default) · Cutoff date (default 2 days, CMS-configurable per event: 1/2/3 days)

Signup Flow

Collect & charge

Fields: first name, last name, email, phone. Mandatory 18+ waiver checkbox (acceptance recorded). Pay full amount up-front via Stripe — single transaction, no deposit/balance complexity.

Class Formats

Two distinct types in the system

Monthly series: e.g. every Wednesday for a month, one design across 4 weeks, can't skip. Pay for the full month at signup. Each month is a different design.

Pop-up events: Seasonal/holiday one-offs. Simpler — pre-cut glass, pick colors, walk out with a finished piece. Single-session.

Capacity & Waitlist

8 max per class (CMS-editable)

Live count of seats remaining shown publicly. When full → switch to "Notify Me" / waitlist email collection. Email captures feed a list Sam can email when seats open or future classes are scheduled.

Email Automation

Transactional emails (built into signup system)

Auto-confirmation on signup. Auto-reminder emails leading up to class (logic TBD — likely T-7, T-2, T-day). Separate from broader email marketing — that's a different platform decision (see content section).

Open Studio Subscription

Monthly subscription unlocks open studio hours

Stripe recurring subscription. Members get a code or login to verify when they walk in. Lower priority than class signup but should be in the spec — likely a Phase 1.5 build.

❓
Open question for proposal: per-class pricing Need ballpark numbers for monthly series and pop-ups before finalizing the signup design. Ask in the proposal email or capture in the walkthrough.

Pricing Strategy & Tiers

Committed: tiered proposal in the $6,000–$12,000 range

Sam wants choice upfront — "this is the quote, Option B saves $X because it's simpler." This is the opposite of chip-giving negotiation. Give her three real options and let her pick what matters.

Tier What's Included Price
Option A — Lean Launch Home, About, Menu (PDF upload), Contact, Gallery, Class signup w/ Stripe + waiver + waitlist, transactional emails. No open studio subscription. No private events landing pages. ~$6,500
Option B — Recommended Everything in A + CMS-editable menu w/ specials display + private events single form w/ event-type dropdown + open studio subscription (Stripe recurring) + email marketing setup & integration. ~$8,500
Option C — Full Build Everything in B + dedicated landing pages per private event type (/bachelor, /birthday, /corporate) with custom inquiry flow + extended polish/animation budget. ~$11,000

Anchor & Positioning

Recommended Tier Option B at ~$8,500 — middle option, captures everything they actually said they wanted
Payment Terms 50% deposit at signature · 50% at launch (standard)
Ongoing (client direct) Vercel ~$20/mo · Stripe transaction fees · Email platform ($10–$30/mo) · Resend (free tier likely sufficient at launch)
Branding NOT Quoted They're handling — confirmed on call. Don't even mention as add-on.
📊
Value framing for proposal Frame it around hours saved and revenue captured. The signup system stops Sam from coordinating signups by DM/email. Waitlist captures revenue that would otherwise walk away. Email automation gets people back through the door for next month's design. This isn't a brochure — it's an operations platform.
