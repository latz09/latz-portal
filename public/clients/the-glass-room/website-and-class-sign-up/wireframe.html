Pay-in-full at signup — no deposit/balance complexity. Mandatory 18+ waiver checkbox with acceptance recorded. Stripe handles payment. Resend handles confirmation + reminder emails (T-7, T-2, T-day — timing TBD). Sam sees who signed up per event in the CMS dashboard.
Capacity & Waitlist Logic
Default: 8 seats per class (CMS-editable per event). Live count shows publicly. When full, the signup button switches to a "Notify Me" / waitlist email capture. Waitlist emails feed a list Sam can use to notify people when new classes are scheduled.
Waitlist isn't auto-promote (someone cancels → next person gets in). It's email capture for Sam to notify manually. Keeps it simple and avoids automated refund/recharge complexity.
Two Class Formats in the System
CMS managed
Monthly Series
Every Wednesday for a month · one design across 4 weeks · can't skip · pay for the full month at signup. Each month is a different design. This is the core offering.
Pop-Up Events
Seasonal/holiday one-offs · pre-cut glass · pick colors · walk out with a finished piece · single session. Simpler format, lower price point, great for first-timers.
Both formats use the same signup system — Sam creates the event in CMS, sets dates, capacity, cutoff, price, and description. The system handles the rest. Walk-in craft days don't need online booking — those are in-store only.
