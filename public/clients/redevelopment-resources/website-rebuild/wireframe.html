Modal Cards vs. Dedicated Case-Study Pages
Option A — Modal Expandable Cards
Every case study opens in an expandable card or modal on this page. Faster to build and maintain, keeps everything in one place.
Option B — Dedicated Pages
The strongest 3–4 stories each get a full dedicated page with its own URL — better for SEO and for sharing a single story directly.
Recommendation: dedicated pages at minimum for the Irish Hospital / East Elementary story and the Fitchburg / Elkhorn testimonials — this is the strongest material on the whole site and deserves its own URL. Remaining stories can use the lighter modal-card treatment. Final call to be confirmed with Kristen.
