⟶ How Purchasing Works
The Buying Experience
What your customers see when they order a book — from "Add to Cart" to delivery.
🛒 The shopping experience is built right into your site — customers never feel like they've left. Behind the scenes, Shopify handles the payment processing and your distributor (Itasca/Bookmobile) handles fulfillment. You never touch inventory or ship anything.
Step 1 — Browse & Add to Cart
Where it happens: On your site — jeanninekellogg.com
Customer is on a book page or the Shop page. They click "Add to Cart" — the book gets added to their cart without leaving the page. Everything looks and feels like your site because it is your site.

A cart drawer slides in from the right side of the screen. Shows what's in the cart, quantities, and the total. Customer can update quantities, remove items, or click "Checkout" to proceed.

Step 3 — Secure Checkout
Where it happens: Shopify's secure checkout page
When the customer clicks "Checkout," they go to a secure Shopify-hosted payment page. This is the only moment they leave your site — and it's branded with your store name and colors so it still feels connected. They enter shipping address, payment info, and confirm the order.
Shopify handles all payment processing, tax calculation, and order confirmation emails.

Step 4 — Order Confirmed & Fulfilled
What happens next — behind the scenes:
✅
Customer gets an order confirmation email immediately from Shopify.
📦
Within the hour, Itasca Books (via Bookmobile) automatically picks up the order from Shopify.
🚚
Itasca picks, packs, and ships the book directly to the customer. You never touch inventory.
💰
Payment goes to your Shopify account. You get paid — Itasca handles their fulfillment fees separately through your existing arrangement.
This is the same automated fulfillment pipeline that Bookmobile runs for publishers on Shopify. Their system pulls orders every hour — no manual work on your end. The connection between Shopify and Itasca is a proven, established process.
The Bottom Line
Your customers browse and shop on your beautiful custom site. Shopify handles payment. Itasca/Bookmobile handles shipping. You manage products in a simple Shopify dashboard — add books, update prices, track orders. The monthly cost is $39/mo for Shopify Basic. That's it.
