01 A Note From Jordan
Jeannine,

You came back from something that would have ended most publishing efforts. Lost a 2,400-book customer, survived a catastrophic print run, went quiet for months — and then reached out on your own, ready to relaunch a trilogy. That tells me everything I need to know about what this project deserves.

The v1 site gave you legitimacy. Parents met you at book fairs, searched your name, and found a real author with a real site. But it can't sell a book. That's the gap — and closing it is the entire point of v2.

This proposal covers a complete 9-page rebuild: dedicated pages for each book in the trilogy, Shopify-powered ecommerce with Itasca/Bookmobile fulfillment built in, a CMS so you can manage book details and toggle availability yourself, and full integration of the new Fuzzy Duck branding as assets arrive. Your existing Madsen artwork carries over. We write the web copy for every page. When a parent searches your name after a school visit, they'll find a site with a buy button.

You already reviewed the wireframe — this is the formal scope and investment breakdown. One price, no surprises, and you own it all.

04 How It Works
1
Deposit & Kickoff
50% deposit locks in the project. I'll set up the Shopify store, add your books, send you login credentials, and get the email to Hans at Itasca started so Bookmobile can connect to your store.
2
Design & Build
The wireframe is done — that's a major head start. I'll build from it, integrating Fuzzy Duck brand assets as they arrive. You'll have a live development link to follow along in real time. Shopify cart and checkout get wired in during this phase.
3
Review & Refine
You review the live site. We go through it together, make adjustments until it's right. We'll run a test order end-to-end — purchase on the site, Bookmobile receives it, confirms fulfillment. No guessing, no surprises.
4
Launch & Handoff
Domain connected, site live, Book 1 for sale. Final 50% due at launch. CMS training so you can toggle book availability, update content, and manage reviews yourself. Books 2 & 3 go live when they're ready — you flip the switch, not me. 3 months of support included.
Timeline: The wireframe is mapped, the ecommerce plan is set, and we know the platform. Realistic build is 6–8 weeks from deposit to launch — with flexibility built in for Fuzzy Duck deliverables and Madsen's illustration schedule. Book 1 can go live as soon as it's ready; Books 2 & 3 launch on your timeline.
