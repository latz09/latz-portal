03 Why This Site Matters

You serve three completely different audiences through one front door. Real estate agents want speed — book and go. Branding clients need to feel the vibe before they reach out. Family clients want to feel welcome. Right now, all three hit the same GoDaddy page and are left to figure it out themselves.

That's not a small thing. You already lost a lead to a missed GoDaddy chat notification — the site isn't just a portfolio, it's your routing system, your first impression, and the thing that either builds trust or loses the client before they ever reach out.

And you're actively pushing into branding work this year. You need a site that supports who you're becoming — not just who you've been. That's exactly what Stage 1 starts and Stage 2 completes.
