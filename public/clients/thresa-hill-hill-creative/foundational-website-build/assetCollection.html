02

A Quick Note on Photos

📷
You're the photographer here — this isn't a lecture. Just a couple of file-handling notes so your work shows up on the web exactly as good as it does on your screen. Your images literally are the site, so these are the only things that really matter.
Photo Tips
✅ Helps a lot
  • Send the originals — straight from your catalog, not downloaded from social
  • A few horizontal shots per category for the hero / banner areas
  • RE: mix of wide room shots and details
  • Branding: a range of session types
  • Lifestyle: families, seniors, couples — show the range
  • File names like re-kitchen-01.jpg help, but no stress
⚠️ Worth avoiding
  • Social-media exports (they get compressed and lose quality)
  • Screenshots of your own photos
  • Tiny / resized copies — full-res is always better
  • Vertical-only sets for a category (we need some horizontals for heros)
