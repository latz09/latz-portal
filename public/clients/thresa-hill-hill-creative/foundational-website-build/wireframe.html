What Happens on Shoot Day
When I arrive for your photoshoot, the home should be photo-ready, but I'll still help with the final touches — I'll turn on all the lights, put down toilet lids, and tidy small things like countertop clutter. The rest is up to you, so check the provided checklist beforehand.
Feel free to head out for coffee and leave me entry instructions, or hang out on another floor while I work. I'll go through the home three times — once for photos, once for video, and once for a 360° tour if you've signed up for it. Depending on size and services, this can take 45 minutes to 2 hours — great time to grab a coffee with your clients.
