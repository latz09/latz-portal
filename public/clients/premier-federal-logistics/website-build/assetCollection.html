02

A Note on Photos

📷
For a federal-facing site, real photography makes all the difference. Stock imagery can fill gaps, but nothing builds credibility like actual photos of your team and operations. Whether it's from a professional shoot or solid phone photos, here's what's most useful.
Photo Tips
✅ Most useful
  • Professional headshots of Tracy and Bill
  • Equipment, trucks, or fleet shots
  • Operations or field work in progress
  • Horizontal / landscape orientation
  • Well-lit — outdoors or bright natural light
  • Anything that shows the scale and seriousness of your work
⚠️ Try to avoid
  • Screenshots from Facebook or social media
  • Very dark or blurry shots
  • Heavily filtered or edited photos
  • Portrait / vertical phone photos (for hero use)
  • Tiny file sizes — send the originals

Domain Info

🌐
We'll need access to premierfederallogistics.com to connect it to the new site. Don't worry — this is a routine step and we'll walk you through everything. You stay in control of the domain at all times.
What We Need to Know
  • 🔍
    Where is the domain registered?
    We know premierfederallogistics.com is active and running Google Workspace for email. The domain is registered somewhere — GoDaddy, Namecheap, Google Domains (now Squarespace Domains), or through your Google Workspace setup. The best way to find out: check your inbox for the original domain registration confirmation email, or search your email for "premierfederallogistics.com." You can also type the domain into who.is or do a quick Google search for "whois premierfederallogistics.com" — it'll tell you right away. Getting familiar with where it lives now will make the connection step much smoother later.
  • 🔑
    Login credentials
    Once you've tracked down the registrar, locate your login credentials and keep them handy. We'll reach out when it's time to connect the domain and figure out the quickest path from there. Your domain stays yours — nothing gets transferred.
  • 📧
    Google Workspace — no changes needed
    Your existing Google Workspace email (info@premierfederallogistics.com) will continue working through the transition. The contact form on the new site will route directly to your inbox. No interruption to email service.
